How to read this. Your band compares your numbers to each school's median class, weighting LSAT more than GPA. Likely = at or above the class profile, Target = competitive near the median, Reach = below the median but in range, Hard reach = below the 25th percentile. "Employment" is the share of graduates in full-time, long-term jobs requiring or preferring a JD. "Big firm" is full-time long-term jobs at firms of 251+ attorneys. "Bar passage" is the school's first-time pass rate. "Net tuition" subtracts the school's median grant from one year of tuition, a rough estimate only.

Source: official ABA Standard 509 Required Disclosures, 2025 (admissions, grants, tuition, employment) and 2026 first-time bar admission. Some schools do not report every field; missing values show as a dash. This is a positioning and research tool, not an admissions prediction or financial advice. Verify any figure against each school's own ABA disclosure before relying on it.
